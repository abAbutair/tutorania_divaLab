Privacy Policy

Tutorania is a digital community for the private trainers and freelance instructors and coaches, who can conduct private training sessions for people who are interested to learn about a certain subject in ANY field. If you are a tutor, you will be able to see the learners’ requests, and submit proposals for them. Additionally, you will be able to get reviews and rating from the learners as a feedback. Finally, you will still be able to post a request to learn a certain topic, as if you are a “Learner”.
Tutorania is based on the idea of social networking. The website managers don’t create the content; it is created by the page subscribers.
At registration, you will need to choose one of two options: either you are a “Learner”, or you are a “Tutor”.

At registration

If you are a Learner, you will be able to log on the website, search for tutors of specific category, or in a geographical area, and select the tutor you need. Or you can simply submit a request that you want to learn a certain subject or skill, and the tutors registered will submit proposals that include time ,duration and fees to provide you these private sessions.
If you are a tutor, you will be able to see the learners’ requests, and submit proposals for them. Additionally, you will be able to get reviews and rating from the learners as a feedback. Finally, you will still be able to post a request to learn a certain topic, as if you are a “Learner”.
  • Open community for private trainers and freelance instructors.
  • Subscription Free of charge
  • Tutorania covers all subjects’ fields
  • Tutorania is not restricted to specific geography, it is EVERYWHERE !
